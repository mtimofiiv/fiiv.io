30 Wears

Originally started by Olivia Firth of Eco-Age, the #30wearschallenge is a social challenge to wear garments 30 times or more.

I built a mobile app to facilitate this for a eco hackathon. You could add your garments, and track how many times you wear them.

It really took off! A lot of people used it - about 2 thousand active users, in fact. But unfortunately, I just don't have the time to maintain it, so I had to take the sad step to discontinue it.

Thank you to all our users!

Status: shut down
Technologies used: Node.js, PostgreSQL, Expo, React Native, Docker
